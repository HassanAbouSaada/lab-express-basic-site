Norman Foster's Works

The Dome in Berlin

The Dome in Berlin is a dome-shaped glass building that was constructed to mark the reunification of Germany. It was completed in 2000 and is now a popular tourist attraction. The building has a diameter of 365 feet and stands over 270 feet tall.

The Philological Library in Berlin

The Philological Library in Berlin is part of the Free University of Berlin and houses the university's literature and linguistics collection. The library was completed in 2005 and is known for its unique design. The building has four floors and is clad in glass and steel.

The Gherkin Tower in London

The Gherkin Tower, also known as 30 St Mary Axe, is a skyscraper in the financial district of London. It was completed in 2003 and is one of the most recognizable buildings in London. The building is 180 meters tall and has 41 floors.
